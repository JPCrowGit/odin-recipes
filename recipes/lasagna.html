Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian recipe containing tomato sauce and bechemel or ricotta cheese layered between sheets of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bechemel sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Make red sauce
  2. Boil pasta sheets
  3. Layer red sauce, bechemel or mozarella, and pasta sheets
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes

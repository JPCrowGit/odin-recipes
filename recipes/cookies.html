Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar together
  2. Add eggs and mix
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and baking soda
  4. Add flour mixture to egg and butter mixture
  5. Mix chocolate chips until just combined
  6. Portion cookies onto a baking sheet and bake at 300 degrees for 15-20 minutes
